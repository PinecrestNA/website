Safe Streets for Pinecrest

In the past eight years there have been 187 traffic accidents in our neighborhood and 9 fatalities. We have all observed traffic driving too fast through our neighborhood...even near the park and the school. The City of Tucson has a Safe Streets Mini-Grant Program that pays for traffic calming features in neighborhoods. We are thinking we'd like an additional speed hump on Arcadia and two speed humps on Fairmount.
